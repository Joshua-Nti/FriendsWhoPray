At the Feet of Jesus

Every Wednesday, 9:00AM-9:30PM GMT Online

Join us for a 30-minute prayer meeting where we will pour out our hearts before Jesus, reminding Him of our inheritance in Him and boldly claiming it. This is a sacred time to bring our petitions before God—whether they relate to career, health, marriage, relationships, ministry, or other areas of life. The final 10 minutes will be dedicated to making declarations from the Word of God, standing firm in faith, and claiming the promises that are rightfully ours as His children.

Stretch prayers

3rd Saturday of Every Month, 9:00PM-1:00AM GMT Online

Stretch Prayer is an extended prayer session designed to help us dwell in God's presence for longer hours, saturating ourselves in His aura and fragrance as we prepare for the journey ahead. This time of intense prayer pushes us beyond our physical limits, allowing us to encounter His power more deeply and touch the hem of His garment. Despite any distractions or obstacles, we press in to experience the fullness of His presence.

About Us

Friends Who Pray (FWP) is a Christian movement dedicated to sharing the love of Jesus Christ through compassionate outreach. We empower communities by providing health services, promoting environmental stewardship, fostering sustainable development, and spreading the gospel.Rooted in prayer and action, we seek to transform lives by addressing both spiritual and physical needs. Our vision is to build thriving, Christ-centered communities, all for the Glory of God.Join us in making a difference, bringing hope, healing, and the Good News to those in need
