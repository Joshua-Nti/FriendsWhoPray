About Us

Welcome to Friends Who Pray (FWP) Est. Since 2019

Friends Who Pray (FWP) is a vibrant Christian movement committed to sharing the love and gospel of Jesus Christ through compassionate outreach. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to transform lives by addressing the holistic needs of communities—spiritually, physically, and socially.

We believe that faith, action, and service are the pillars of true discipleship. Guided by the teachings of Christ, we organize initiatives that provide health outreach, promote environmental conservation, foster sustainable community development, and cultivate spiritual growth. Our vision is to build a future where every individual not only thrives in health and purpose but also experiences the joy and peace of walking with Jesus.

At FWP, we believe in the power of prayer and action. Whether it's providing medical care to underserved populations, teaching sustainable practices, or simply sharing the message of Christ’s love, we are dedicated to empowering communities for the Glory of God. With a heart for evangelism, we are passionate about seeing lives transformed by the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

Join us as we continue to uplift, equip, and share the Good News, impacting lives and glorifying God in everything we do.
